What is UncommonKnowledge?

UNCOMMONKNOWLEDGE is an online course designed to guide aspiring prospects to free themselves from the pants to the lance!

In other words, if you want to become your own boss and earn some dosh, UNCOMMONKNOWLEDGE will inform and provide you with the secrets to success!

The Mission

Our mission is to educate those from various backgrounds about business and guide them through the start up process whether it be for freelance or a full-blown business.

The Vision

To inspire, educate, equip and support the community to provide them with an experience that will prepare them for success.
